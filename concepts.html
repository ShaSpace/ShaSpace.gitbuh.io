Smart Runway

The SmartRunway is a way for stylists, designers, and those involved in the fashion world to take clothing designs to the next level. The runway senses the model position on the runway and triggers a lighting event. The runway itself may glow, either the single tile or the entire runway, or overhead lighting may trigger as well. The color of the light can be configured, and trigger at each footstep the model takes. Ideally, a venue would rent out the runway at the artists' request. Keeping that in mind, the runway would be able to break down easily for a more efficient way of travel.

Other Applications

  • Dance Floor
    The settings can be adjusted so that lights can trigger if anyone steps in a designated area. This would be especially useful for themed parties to help bring in more customers, and just add to the environment in general.
  • Laser Tag
    Laser tag is already an activity that rely heavily on the balance between light and dark. With the Smart Runway application venues can figure the runway to light up when someone is out, or just to add another element to the activity.

View Catcher

The idea behind the View Catcher, is to redirect a person's attention to the object of attraction. For example, take an art exhibit with shiny or reflective material. As a visitor walks by, the motion would trigger a light to shine on the exhibit. The reflection would catch the person's attention, giving the exhibit viewing time. It would also help the visitor feel more apart of the experience, they could walk back and forth to see at least two different versions of the art piece.

Other Applications

  • Entertainment
    I think adding this to indoor mazes, or perhaps a horror attraction would be a cool application for the View Catcher. If someone is standing still but see the lights activate they would know someone is coming and it would further the experience.
